Move Out & Donate

The Ask

Get students to donate the dorm furniture they've coexisted with for the past 9 months.

The Answer

Make it an easy breakup and let your unwanted alarm clock, tv, or couch live a second life. We brought recycling to the point of trash conversion, reminding

Execution

Posters were placed at points of conversion, like dumpsters and dorm building entrances. Social images were posted to JWU official accounts on instagram, twitter, and facebook.

The Results

This campaign was used by Johnson & Wales University for a month at the end of the school year. Campaign as a whole was covered by multiple local news networks.
