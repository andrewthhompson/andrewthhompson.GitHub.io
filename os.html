Ocean Spray
Plansbook

Ask

Create a fully integrated campaign to get millenials to eat and drink cranberries. Oh, and make it snappy.

Answer

Show them how they can add different to their day, from cran-orange drinks in the morning to craisins for the after-work commute.

Execution

We emphasized the power of the cranberry to change your day by adding in a "nimbus" around models and products alike. Creative shown is a combination of unused concepts and finished work.

Results

Final campaign was presented as part of the 2018 NSAC. Placed 1st in New England District Competition, and 9th nationally in the Semifinals.
