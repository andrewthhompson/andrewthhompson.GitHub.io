The Ask

Rebrand a government entity with no brand image and a logo made in Microsoft Publisher. Bring the department from the 19th centurn into the 21st with a brand image that screams cutting edge.

The Answer

We crafted a logo that links the towns of lil Rhody by silicon, emphasizing a commitement to reconnecting communities with cutting edge mental health medicine.

Execution

The towns of Rhode Island are interconnected by silicon-like links, denoting a commitment to the future of mental health medicine. Horizontal lines are introduced as an element that allows the logo to adapt to a variety of placements.

The Results

Rebrand was presented to the client. They are currently in the process of choosing their new brand identity from a pool presented by students from this class.
