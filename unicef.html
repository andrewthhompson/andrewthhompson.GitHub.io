UNICEF Kid Power Bands
Slidedeck

The Ask

Convince parents to buy Unicef Kid Power Bands for their children for their own sake and that of kids world-wide.

The Answer

Show them how their kids can change the world from as far away as the park down the block.

Execution

We split the difference between portraying the places that children play and the children themselves, emphasizing the idea of "kid power". Outdoor ads are more visual focused, while magazine ads are supported by body copy.

The Results

Received a near-perfect grade. This campaign is now an example of student work for the Johnson & Wales University Advertising program.
