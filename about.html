Hi, I'm Andrew, and I like to think I can design. (Hey, my Mom says I can, so screw you art school). I have an unhealthy love for RGB hex codes, Muji, and Italian soda from Trader Joes. You can usually find me with a coffee in my hand and BBQ in my stomach, trying to find a place to park that doesn't cost an arm and a leg—without parallel parking. I think of myself as a minimalist urbanist, which is a way of saying that I don't care how small Providence is. And, most of all, I'm passionate about history and finding out where things come from (and how they got here).
This headshot was taken at 6 in the morning at a diner in the ever-gritty Port of Providence after an all nighter. Ignore the look of dispair.
